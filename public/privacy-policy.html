Privacy Policy for FretPath

Effective date: 26 June 2026

FretPath ("the App") is a guitar learning app developed by Tool Shift ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains what information the App handles and how, in plain language.

The short version: FretPath does not have a server. Nothing you do in the App is sent anywhere. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

FretPath stores the following information locally on your device only, using your device's standard app storage. We never receive, see, or have access to this data:

  • Profile information you enter during setup — your name, guitar type, practice goals, and musical influences.
  • Learning progress — completed lessons, XP, streaks, badges earned, and practice history.

This data is used solely to personalize your experience within the App (e.g., tracking which lessons you've completed, showing your XP). It is never transmitted over the internet, because the App does not connect to any server for this purpose.

You can export this data to a JSON file at any time from Settings, or permanently delete it using the "Reset All Progress" option in Settings. Uninstalling the App also deletes all of this data.

2. Microphone Access

FretPath uses your device's microphone for two features: the chromatic tuner, and verifying that you've correctly played a note, chord, or song progression as part of a lesson.

Audio from your microphone is analyzed in real time, directly on your device, and is never recorded, saved, transmitted, or stored in any form — by us or anyone else. The App only extracts pitch/frequency information momentarily to compare against the expected note or chord; it does not retain any audio data once that comparison is made.

You will be asked to grant microphone permission the first time you use the tuner or a practice-verification feature. You can decline or revoke this permission at any time in your device's app settings — doing so will only disable the tuner and verification features; the rest of the App will continue to work normally.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties, because no data leaves your device.
  • We do not require or offer account creation or sign-in.

4. Children's Privacy

FretPath does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly target children under 13 with any data-collection practices, since none exist.

5. Permissions Summary (Android)

  • Microphone — required for the tuner and lesson verification features, as described in Section 2.
  • Internet — currently unused by the App's core functionality at this time but may be declared by the underlying app framework; the App does not make use of network access to transmit your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version of the App adds optional cloud sync or online features — we will update this page and revise the effective date above. We will not retroactively reduce your privacy without clear notice.
